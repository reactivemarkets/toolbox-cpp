MaxMsgSize 

Maximum message size. A sensible upper-bound for message payloads is 1400 bytes:

  • Max Datagram (1472) - Aeron Header (32) = 1440
  • Round-down to cache-line boundary: (1440 & ~63) = 1408
  • Subtract MPMC queue header: 1408 - 8 = 1400

◆ parse_time()

noexcept

Time/date combination represented in UTC (Universal Time Coordinated, also known as "GMT") in either YYYYMMDD-HH:MM:SS (whole seconds) or YYYYMMDD-HH:MM:SS.sss (milliseconds) format, colons, dash, and period required.

Valid values:

  • YYYY = 0000-9999;
  • MM = 01-12;
  • DD = 01-31;
  • HH = 00-23;
  • MM = 00-59;
  • SS = 00-60 (60 only if UTC leap second);
  • sss = 000-999 (indicating milliseconds).
